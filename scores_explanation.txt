1. g_cost is the cost of getting from Paul to the cake
It assumes that we can only travel down and to the right.
The squares that are adjacent shows the cost of movement if you move to that specific square.

2. h_cost is the cost of getting from the cake to Paul
It assumes that we can only travel upward and to the left.
The squares that are adjacent shows the cost of movement if you move to that specific square.

3. f_cost is the sum of g_cost and h_cost
